Shipping Information

How will my parcel be delivered?

Your parcel will be carefully packaged and shipped via our reliable shipping partners, ensuring safe and timely delivery to your doorstep. We offer a range of delivery options, including standard shipping and expedited shipping, to accommodate your preferences and urgency.

Do I pay for delivery?

Whether you need to pay for delivery depends on several factors, including your location, the total value of your order, and any ongoing promotions or discounts. We offer free standard shipping on orders over a certain amount, which may vary depending on your region. For orders below this threshold, a shipping fee will apply. Additionally, expedited shipping options may incur an additional cost. Rest assured, we strive to offer transparent and competitive shipping rates to ensure a seamless shopping experience for our customers.

My item has become faulty!

We apologize for any inconvenience caused by a faulty item. If you receive a defective product, please contact our customer service team immediately to initiate the return and exchange process. In your message, provide details about the issue, including any relevant photos or videos, to help us assess the problem accurately. Once we receive your request, we will guide you through the necessary steps.
